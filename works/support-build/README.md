# Support Build

**Status:** Active — drafting Arc 1.

**Premise (spoiler-free):** Twenty-two years after the Gates opened and the System arrived, everything an awakened person can do is ranked by damage output on the Gate League's leaderboards. Ren Okafor awakens as an *Attendant* — buffs, escort skills, and a Potential Index of "unindexed" — a class the assessors openly call a butler. The only team desperate enough to sign him is Grave Society: five members, one league win ever, rank 214 of 215, and four days from forfeiting their season for want of a sixth body. But Ren reads skill text more carefully than anyone in the league, and the System's text is exact — including the parts nobody bothers to read.

**Steering regime:** Owner is hands-off: AI plans and writes everything; owner reads published chapters spoiler-free (never opens `plan/`) and gives direction/feedback between chapters, logged in `log.md`.

**Voice:** clear, scene-first, anime-fantasy web serial per the authors-voice skill — leaderboards, named skills, honest tactics, banter that stops when stakes arrive.
